# About

<img src="{{ '/files/profile.jpeg' | relative_url }}" alt="Portrait of Manish Dutta" class="profile-image">

I am a researcher interested in autocratization, democratic backsliding, international organisations, global social policy, decolonisation, and human rights.

Currently, I am open to PhD opportunities, and my work focuses on changes in governance in the era of democratic backsliding.

I was born and brought up in Kolkata (old name Calcutta), and I finished my schooling there. I completed my BA in Political Science from Presidency University, Kolkata, India. In 2021, I moved to Germany, also the first time I moved out of my home town. In Germany, I moved to Bremen to study at the University of Bremen.
I got my MA in International Relations with an overall grade of 1,78.
My master's thesis was on exploring the warfare-welfare nexus in Poland. For more details on the thesis, please see my publications.
